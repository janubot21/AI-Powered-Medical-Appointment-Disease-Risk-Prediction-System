About This Project

AIHealth Hub is an AI-assisted healthcare support system for risk-aware appointment planning. It evaluates patient health inputs using historical and engineered clinical features, and provides practical insights for triage support, appointment prioritization, and care follow-up.

Primary Goal

Risk Forecast

Scope

Patient-Level

Output Horizon

Instant/Visit-Based

How The Project Works

  • Collects patient profile, vitals, and symptom signals from portal workflows.
  • Transforms raw health entries into model-ready risk features.
  • Runs trained AI models to classify disease risk into actionable levels.
  • Shows doctors and patients a consistent summary for appointment decisions.
  • Supports admin-controlled data updates and model-driven screening flows.

Data Used By The Model

Current dataset: backend/datasets/Healthcare_FeatureEngineered.csv

Date & Identity patient id, age, gender, profile linkage, visit context
Vitals & Risk Signals blood pressure, glucose, bmi, clinical flags, symptom counts
Symptom Indicators fatigue, chest pain, breathlessness, fever, headache, and encoded symptom categories
Derived Features engineered risk buckets, normalized symptom tokens, and model-ready encoded attributes

How To Measure "How Much Better"

Improvement should be measured against your current manual/heuristic triage baseline over the same patient window.

  • Track model error and classification quality: precision, recall, F1, and calibration.
  • Compare missed high-risk cases and unnecessary urgent referrals before vs after adoption.
  • Track appointment quality impact: faster high-risk routing and more stable doctor workload.

In this project, "better" means safer prioritization, fewer avoidable misses, and improved consistency in clinical decision support.
